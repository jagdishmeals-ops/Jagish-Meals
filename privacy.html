2.3 Payment information we do not collect

Card numbers, CVV codes, and bank account details are entered directly into Stripe's secure payment interface. We never see, store, or transmit your raw card data. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified. We only receive a payment confirmation token and the amount paid.

3. How we use your information

  • Provide the Service: Create your account, deliver tiffins, process wallet top-ups, place orders, send order confirmations.
  • Communicate with you: Send transactional notifications (order placed, order delivered, wallet topped up). With your permission, we may send promotional notifications about new menus or offers.
  • Improve the Service: Analyze general usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features. We do not sell your usage data.
  • Comply with the law: Respond to lawful requests from authorities, prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data — most fields can be edited directly in the app's Profile section.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and personal data. We will retain transactional records as required by tax law (see Section 6).
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to specific uses of your data, such as marketing notifications (you can also disable notifications in your device settings).
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for any processing that relies on it. This will not affect processing already completed.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 13. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

Indian users: These rights are also protected under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

EU/UK users: If you reside in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Security

We take commercially reasonable security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS 1.2+) and at rest (Google Cloud and Firestore default encryption).
  • Authentication via Google Sign-In OAuth — we never store your password.
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing via Stripe — we never see your card details.
  • Server-side validation of all wallet and order operations to prevent client-side tampering.
  • Idempotency keys on payment processing to prevent double-charging.

No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

9. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

For users in jurisdictions where the minimum age for data processing is higher (for example, 16 in some EU member states under GDPR), we apply that higher age threshold.

10. Cookies and similar technologies
